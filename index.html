About Us

Established on the Oudekerksplein you’ll find a cutting-edge and incomparable eatery run by a crazy bunch of passionate people from Italy. A mix of contemporary and the modern Italian cuisine, where excellent wines and groundbreaking cocktails join forces. Lolo is out of the ordinary, yet homelike. Joyfull, lush and funky at night, with house made playlists and a homelike vibe.

Chi mangia solo crepa solo

Welcome to Lolo's Italian restaurant, where we passionately bring the flavors of Italy to life. Dishes such as creamy burrata, risotto al merluzzo, and papardelle napolitana let you enjoy la dolce vita.

Our head chef combines traditional cooking techniques with modern influences, all made from fresh Italian ingredients.

Italy is not only tasted in our dishes but also in our cocktails. For the aficionados, we have unique creations like Limon Tette, a Baciami, or a Negroni - classic or with a twist. At Lolo, the quality of taste is paramount, both in our dishes and in Lolo’s cocktails.
